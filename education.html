My Education Journey

Education has been my compass, pointing me to places I never imagined I would go. Each step has been a learning experience, shaping my perspective and honing my skills.

Lugulu AC Primary School (2004 - 2011)

My academic life began at Lugulu AC Primary School in 2004. Here, I learned my ABCs and 123s, foundational lessons that built the bedrock of my knowledge. I graduated in 2011, carrying with me wisdom beyond my years and a thirst for further learning.

Malanga Mixed Secondary School (2012 - 2015)

From 2012 to 2015, I joined Malanga Mixed Secondary School. This period was crucial for cutting my teeth on life lessons both inside and outside the classroom, navigating adolescence while striving for academic excellence.

Diploma in Business Information Technology, Zetech University (Jan 2017 - Nov 2018)

After high school, I embarked on my university journey at Zetech University in January 2017, pursuing a Diploma in Business Information Technology. I graduated in November 2018, wearing my graduation gown like a warrior’s armor, proud of the battles fought and won in my pursuit of IT knowledge.

Professional Experiences & Internships

  • Busia Huduma Centre (June – September 2018): My attachment here gave me my first real taste of professional life, applying classroom knowledge to real-world scenarios.
  • Huduma Namba Project (2019) & Oral Genealogy (2019): Engaged in significant national projects, broadening my practical experience.
  • ICS College Nairobi (Early 2020): Worked as a customer care agent, enhancing my communication and problem-solving skills, though this role was interrupted by the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • Opyacomp Cyber (October 2021 – July 2022): Switched gears and gained valuable experience in a cybercafe environment.
  • Digital Literacy Programme Intern, Ministry of Education, Butula Sub-County Office (Feb 2023 – Feb 2024): This internship deepened my understanding of digital education initiatives.

Bachelor’s Degree in Software Engineering, Zetech University (Online, Year 3 Semester 1 - Current)

Currently, I volunteer at the Ministry of Education, Butula Sub-County Office, while pursuing a Bachelor’s Degree in Software Engineering at Zetech University through their online program. This continuous journey has taught me that education is not just about filling the pail but lighting a fire, constantly seeking new knowledge and applying it to make an impact.
