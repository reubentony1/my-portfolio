Hello, I'm Reuben Tony Omondi!

I was born at the crack of dawn, 5:36 a.m., on the 19th of December 1996, in the humble village of Namudoda, Emukhweso sub-location, Elugulu Location, Butula Constituency, Busia County, Kenya.

Life has been a tapestry sometimes threaded with joy, sometimes with tears but always weaving a story worth telling. I wear my faith in the Almighty like a crown, ministering at Full Gospel Churches of Kenya, Malanga Branch, Nambale LCA, Busia District. I believe life is a dance; when it plays a slow song, we sway with patience, and when it beats hard, we dance with passion.

My ultimate goal is to become a technocrat in software development, crafting solutions that solve real-world problems and inspire the next generation, while also continuing my passion for farming. Feel free to explore the different sections to learn more about my journey!
